    AGNAKTOR


    Agnaktor is a leviathan large monster. It looks similar to Lagiacrus and he is covered in lava that becomes harder when cooled. It uses his head as a drill to go underground.


    "Also known as fire-pike wyverns, Agnaktor use their tough beaks and great strength to burrow through rock, and sometimes even cave ceilings. Encrusted with hardened lava that can soften from the heat when they spit magma or burrow."


    Agnaktor appears in:

    • 5★ Village quest Denizem of the Molten Deep
    • 6★ Urgent Village quest A Burnt Offering
    • 3★ Low Rank Online quest [Advanced] Agnaktor Alert
    • 5★ High Rank Online quest The Molten Monstrosity and Agnaktor Alert
    • 6★ High Rank Online quest A Burnt Offering
