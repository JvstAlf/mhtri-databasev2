Longsword

The Longsword is a weapon class in Monster Hunter Tri known for the agile and fast movements and an high attack power.
The defining trait of the longsword is the Spirit Gauge. Using the Z button on the Wiimote, unleash a special combo of attacks that leads to a new level of Spirit Gauge when endind the combo with the Spirit Roundslash.
Theres 3 levels of spirit gauge: White, Yellow and Red; each level increases attack and lasts less than the previous one.
Unlike other weapons, the longsword is not avaiable at the beginning of the game.
